  • Texas A&M Big Event

    The Big Event at Texas A&M University is a large, student-run community service project where thousands of Aggies volunteer in the local community. Each year, students participate in various service activities, such as yard work, painting, and cleaning, to express gratitude and give back to residents of Bryan College Station. The event reflects Texas A&M's commitment to selfless service and community engagement. I saw my friends participating at the Big Event last year, but I was too late to apply.   • Aggieland Human Society

    Volunteering with Aggieland Humane Society involves assisting with the care and well-being of animals in the Bryan College Station area. The volunteering work consist of tasks like walking dogs, socializing cats, cleaning kennels, and supporting adoption events. The goal of these works are to improve the lives of animals while promoting responsible pet ownership and community involvement.
